Running

Growing up, I never wanted to be a runner like my sister or my dad or my mom. I didn't see the point of it and thought that I would never truly enjoy it, especially after running multiple holiday 5ks with my family. But once I stopped competitively dancing after my freshman year, I knew I had to pick up another type of sport that would keep me active and give me a loyal community, and thats how I ended up running cross country and track in high school. Below, there is a list of all of the running events I competed in during my cross country and track seasons throughout high school.

Running Events

  • 5k (cross-country)
  • 100m Hurdles
  • 300m Hurdles
  • 400m Dash
  • 4x400m Relay
  • 200m Dash (Indoor Track)
  • 50m Hurdles (Indoor Track)
