Snowboarding

Being from Michigan, I started skiing at the age of 6. It became a yearly activity that our parents practically forced us into. I began taking skii lessons with one of my childhood best friends named Allie, and before we knew it we were out on double black diamonds alone at the age of 10. As our yearly ski trips continued, Allie and I started to get slightly bored of skiing. That's when we decided to pick up snowboarding. We both took one lesson each and then from there we taught each other ways to improve. Now after snowboarding and skiing for almost 14 years, I still look forward to going up north year after year. This year my family has a trip planned that we've been talking about for probably the last 5 years. We will be headed out west to Colorado over Christmas break. We are all so excited for this adventure and to experience the fresh powder the slopes in Colorado have compared to the icy runs we have back home in the mitten.
